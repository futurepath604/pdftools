Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Data Security & Document Processing

We care deeply about your privacy. All document processing (conversion, compression, merging) is transient. Uploaded files are held temporarily in secure application memory and sandbox storage solely during execution. They are completely and permanently purged automatically from our server nodes instantly upon completion.

2. Cookies & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our website. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (such as the DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy.

3. Analytics

We may collect non-personal tracking logs like browser type, language preference, and timestamps to optimize dashboard server capacity. No personally identifiable metrics are collected or processed.
